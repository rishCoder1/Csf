BaSE is a household survey commissioned by Central Square Foundation (CSF) with the aim to bring out the voice of the end-user on the state of EdTech and provide critical insights to stakeholders in the EdTech ecosystem for informed decision-making. This is the first household survey focused on EdTech in low-income settings exploring trends in areas such as access to technology, usage of EdTech, and user sentiment around the adoption of EdTech.
